Study Plan

 published on 12.02.2022
Maxims: Consistency is paramount. 30 minutes of focused study everyday including reading, writing, listening, and speaking. Learn only what you can use now. Avoid specialized/sophisticated words. Create simple routines to make studying easy. Start by building a foundation of: 10 to 20 useful phrases that can be used daily. 400 to 500 most frequent nouns, verbs, adjectives, and adverbs. Question words. 2 or 3 sentence strucutures. Plan: Use paper flashcards. Apps and websites just lead to distraction.
