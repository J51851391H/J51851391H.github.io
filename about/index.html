About

Hi! I’m Jerry. I’m a husband and dad living in Chicago.

My wife was born and raised in Korea and my two kids are both fluent Korean speakers. I’m…working on it.

The truth is, learning Korean is hard. I’ve struggled for years to reach fluency, so I started this blog to share what I know with you. Hopefully my successes, and failures, will help you on your own Korean language journey.
