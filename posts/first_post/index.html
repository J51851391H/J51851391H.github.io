안영 하세요!

Author
 2022-11-11  168 words   One minute 
Contents

I just finished Share Your Work by Austin Kleon. It’s a great (short) book that recommends learning out loud online.

Here’s my favorite tidbits from the book:

Get obsessed with something and spend a ton of time thinking out loud about it.

“The best way to get started on a path…is to think about what you want to learn, and make a commitment to learning it in front of others.”

“Forget about being an expert or a professional, and wear your amateurism (your heart, your love) on your sleeve.”

The “get obsessed” line has stuck with me. I’ve been studying korean for a while now, but I’ve treated it as a second rate hobby rather than an obsession. And that might be the reason by progress has been so slow.

So, I’m going to take Austin’s advice and not only “get obsessed” but do so online. In the weeks and years to come, my plan (hope) is to dive deep into Korean and share my journey here. 화이팅!
