Manners

잘먹겠습니다 = I will eat well.
맛있게 드세요 = Please enjoy your meal.

이거 매워요? = Is this spicy?
요리 너무 잘하세요 = You’re a great cook.

아직 배고파요 = I’m still hungry.
XXX 더 주세요 = Please give me more of XXX
여기 있습니다 = Here you are.

음식 어때요? = How was your meal?
배불러요 = I am full.
잘먹었습니다 = I ate well.

Describing Taste

맛있어요 = It’s delicious
맛 없어요 = It’s not good

매워요 = It’s spicy
너무 매워요. = This is too spicy

써요 = It’s bitter.
짜요 = It’s salty
달아요 = It’s sweet.
셔요 = It’s sour.

신선해요 = It’s fresh.
짭짤해요 = It’s slightly salty.
싱거워요 = It’s bland.

두거워요 = It’s hot.
차가워요 = It’s cold.

맛이 이상해요 = This tastes weird.

맛이 풍부해요 = It is rich in flavor
음식이 상했다 = It’s gone bad
