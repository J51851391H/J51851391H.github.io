음료 vs. 음료수

 11.19.2022 

음료 = drink or beverage
음료수 = tasty beverage

Not all Koreans agree that there’s a distinction between 음료 and 음료수. Most that I’ve spoken to claim, that the words are interchangable in most situations. But, all agreed that 음료 is a general term that refers to all kinds drinks and beverages. Things like water, beer, wine, coffee, tea, etc.

For some, 음료수 is only used to refer to a drink that quenches thurst. Things like vitamin water, soda, or iced tea. Think of a drink you would crave after a long hike on a hot day. That’s an 음료수.
