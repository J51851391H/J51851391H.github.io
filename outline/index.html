Key Phrases

  • 어떻게 지내세요? = How are you?
  • 날씨가 춥지요? = The weather is cold, right?
  • 안녕히 주무셨어요? = Did you sleep well?
  • 오늘 바빠요? = Are you busy today?
  • 드셨어요? = Did you eat?
  • 오늘 계획 있으세요? = What are your plans for today?
  • 안녕히 가세요 = Goodbye (to people leaving)
  • 안녕히 계세요 = Goodbye (when you leave)
  • 몸조심 하세요 = take care of yourself
  • 좋은 하루 되세요 = have a good day!
  • 어때요? = How is it?
  • 저는 이제 가요 = I’m going now
  • 저는 (안) 배고파요 = I’m (not) hungry
  • 저는 (안) 목이 말라요 = I’m (not) thirsty
  • 자는 (안) 피곤해요 = I’m (not) tired
  • 모르겠어요 = I don’t understand
  • 다시 말해 주세요 = please say it again.
  • 맛이 풍부해요 = It’s rich in flavor
  • 밖은 조금 쌀쌀합니다 = It’s a little chilly outside.
  • 밖은 매우 추워요 = It’s very cold outside.
  • 청소하지 마세요. 쉬세요. = Please do not clean. Please rest.
  • 주말 잘 보내세요! = Have a nice weekend
  • 뭐 하고 있었어요? = What have you been doing?
  • 얼씨구 = yipee!
  • 당신도 잘생겼어요 = you are very handsome too (said to a man)
